Depression Assessment

Have you been feeling low, hopeless, or uninterested in things you once enjoyed? This test evaluates mood changes, energy levels, and motivation over the past two weeks. It helps determine whether you may be experiencing mild, moderate, or severe depression.

Have you been feeling down, hopeless, or uninterested in things you usually enjoy?

Do you experience fatigue or trouble concentrating?

Stress & Burnout Test

Are you feeling constantly overwhelmed, drained, or detached from your work or responsibilities? This test measures how well you handle stress in daily life and whether you may be experiencing burnout.

Do you often feel overwhelmed by daily tasks and responsibilities?

Do you feel emotionally drained or detached from your work or personal life?

Social Anxiety & Confidence Test

Do you feel nervous in social settings, avoid conversations, or fear being judged by others? This test helps you understand your comfort level in social interactions and provides strategies for building confidence and reducing social fears.

Do you avoid social situations due to fear of embarrassment or judgment?

Do you feel uncomfortable speaking in front of others?

Emotional Well-being Test

Do you struggle with frequent mood swings, negative thoughts, or emotional ups and downs? This test helps assess your overall mental well-being by measuring depression, anxiety, and stress levels.

Do you frequently experience mood swings or emotional ups and downs?

Do you struggle with negative thoughts that affect your daily life?

OCD Test

Do you experience repetitive thoughts, urges, or compulsive behaviors that interfere with daily life? This test assesses obsessive-compulsive tendencies such as checking, counting, or excessive cleaning. It helps determine if you may need further evaluation or coping strategies.

Do you feel the need to check things repeatedly, even when you know they are fine?

Do unwanted thoughts or images enter your mind and make you feel anxious?

Start Assessment
Addiction Test

Do you find it difficult to control your use of substances like alcohol, nicotine, or drugs? This test helps assess dependency levels, triggers, and coping habits. It provides personalized support strategies to regain control over addictive behaviors.

Do you find it hard to stop using alcohol, nicotine, or other substances, even when you want to?

Do you use substances to cope with stress, anxiety, or emotions?

ADHD Test

Do you struggle with concentration, impulsivity, or restlessness? This test helps identify possible Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) symptoms in adults. It evaluates focus, task management, and hyperactivity levels, offering recommendations for better productivity and focus.

Do you often struggle to focus on tasks or get easily distracted?

Do you frequently forget things like appointments, deadlines, or daily responsibilities?
